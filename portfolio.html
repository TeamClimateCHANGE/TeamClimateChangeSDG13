What is climbirds ?

Climbirds is a non-profit organisation made to help climate change and stop it completely.
We help spread awareness about the situation and what it is and how it impacts us in the form of websites, webinars and online seminars.
And also help run charities to plant trees, clean oceans and do other things that can help slow down climate change.






Why slow down climate change?

If we don’t take action on climate change then the earth’s atmosphere will heat up and so will the soil.
The earth won't be able to support many species of animals and wildlife thus, they will die out.
This will break the delicate balance on which the earth and its ecosystem survives and thus many
more species will die out and human survival will decrease a lot too.
That is why we need to stop climate change and global warming.











How did we help?

Climbirds organised Climacon in December 2019 which aimed to teach ordinary people about global warming
and climate change and help them get to know ways to take action against it.
Along with this many people also had the chance to join our organisation and along with this many celebrities attended the event, too






Earthsavers

When covid struck the world and everyone was in lockdown, we took the chance to spread once more.
We did online webinars that were done in schools while online schooling was going on.
Through these webinars we taught the coming generation about climate change and ways through which they could help. We made them realise
that it was up to them to ensure the stopping of climate change and the fate of our planet.







Charitree

Through this charity, people could donate money ,even as little as 1 rupee, which could be utilised to plant trees.
This charity ran from 2020 to 2021 and during this time people had donated 2 crore rupees and 18 million trees were planted.
This actually helped carbon emissions by quite a bit, and was one our most successful operations.






Global cleanup

Thai charity was to clean all the destruction caused as a side effect of climate change.
This involved cleaning the oceans of trash, cleaning the forest where pollution is happening ,and stopping oil leaks and helping
clean coral reefs and help them grow.
This requires more donations than planting trees, but we got lots of donations and we also used money freely given by our own members.
In total, we cleaned 2 million kilos of trash from the ocean,
cleaned 50 thousand kilos of trash from many forests around the world and stopped around 200 major oil leaks from around the world .
And helps many reefs both small and big, become protected and become larger
